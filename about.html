About Me

Known for its paradise beaches and incredible volcanoes, the vibrant islands of Hawaii are one of the top tourist destinations in the USA. So if you’re looking to learn more then you’re in the right place. I’ve created a few webpages that highlight some of most popular beaches where travelers love to visit that are guaranteed to have you booking your next trip to the Aloha state! Keep in mind that there are so much more to Hawaii than just the places and restaurants that I listed. This is only the start, travelers should research more about what they like to do when visiting Hawaii. There are many resources online, and many other travel guide about on just about all the Hawaiian Islands. Thanks for visiting, and enjoy you’re next vacation trip!

Covid Information !

The culture of our Hawaiian Islands is steeped in the values of kuleana (responsibility) and aloha. Now that travelers can once again enjoy our islands, we are asking you to join in our efforts to help keep Hawaii safe.

Despite the CDC’s announcement on April 2, Hawaii continues with the state’s Safe Travels program, even for fully vaccinated passengers.
